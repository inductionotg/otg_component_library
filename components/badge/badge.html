Badges

Badges can be combined with Avatars, they are useful for status of user like busy, offline and away. They can also be used on icons for notifications.

Badges for Image Avatars

The first variant of Badge is for Round Image Avatars. 

                   	
                   		 <div class="badge" >
                            		< img class="avatar avatar-lg" src="avatar.png" alt="avatar">
                            		< span class="badge-round-avatar badge-lg badge-busy"></span>
                        	</div>
                        	<div class="badge">
                            		<img class="avatar avatar-md" src="avatar.png" alt="avatar">
                            		<span class="badge-round-avatar badge-md badge-away"></span>
                        	</div>
                        	<div class="badge">
                            		<img class="avatar avatar-sm" src="avatar.png" alt="avatar">
                            		<span class="badge-round-avatar badge-sm badge-online"></span >
                        	</div>
                        	<div class="badge">
                            		<img class="avatar avatar-xs" src="avatar.png" alt="avatar">
                            		<span class="badge-round-avatar badge-xs badge-busy"></span>
                        	</div>

Badges for Icons

For Icon Badges 

                	
                		<div class="badge">
                            		<button type="button" class="icon-button">
                            		<i class="fas fa-solid fa-bell fa-lg"></i>
                            		<span class="icon-button__badge">99</span>
                        	</div>
